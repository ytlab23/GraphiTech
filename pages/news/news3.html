SLOPE Final Conference

Graphitech is proud to invite all the interested parties to attend the final SLOPE conference "Precision forestry for mountain timber and biomass production" to be held at the EIMA International, Bologna, Italy, 10 November 2016. The conference will start at 10.00 and will end at 18.00. The program is divided in two sessions: Presentations (Morning) and Workshops (Afternoon).
